About Me

I am a student at the Artificial Intelligence Master's programme at the University of Amsterdam. Over the years I have developed an interest in understanding the nature of intelligence, whether it be by learning about the human mind or by bringing complex behaviour in machines to life.

My passions revolve around science, technology, and philosophy. One of my favourite experiences in life is that of having my world-view changed that occurs after being exposed to new ideas. Through this website, I hope to inspire readers to learn more about these topics, the same way my peers and teachers inspire me.
